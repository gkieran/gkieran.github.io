Absurdity

Monty Python sketches are often somewhat normal interactions turned absurd

  • The Ministry of Silly Walks

    • Brings humor to the seemingly mundane act of walking
    • Political satire on bureaucratic inefficiency and absurd government spending

  • The Argument Clinic

    • A man pays to have an argument, recieves illogical contradictions
    • Turns ordinary arguing into an illogical farce
    • Highlights the absurdity of some human interactions

Social & Political Satire

Monty Python often used comedy to make social and political statements

  • The Dead Parrot

    • A storekeeper tries to convince a man that a (quite obviously)dead parrot
      in his shop is "just resting"
    • Imitates common real-life experiences with uncooperative salespeople

  • The Spanish Inquisition

    • Satirization of religous intolerance
    • Satirical take on a grim historical topic
