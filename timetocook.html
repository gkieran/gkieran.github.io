THE GOD BEVERAGE
  • 2-3 bottles
    5-hour energy, any flavor
  • 1 can
    White Monster
  • 1 bottle
    any flavor Gatorade
  • any amount
    Pill-style edible (HashTabs, etc)
  • a few
    Jolly Ranchers or other hard candy (optional)
  • some
    vodka or gin (optional, if you want to get REALLY shitfaced)
  • 1-2 Scoops
    Preworkout, if you're REALLY that crazy I guess
to a large water bottle, first add the 5 Hour Energy, then the Monster, then top it off with as much of the Gatorade as can fit.
Add the Alcohol and candy if you have any, and crush the edible between 2 spoons and stir it in
close the cop tightly and shake for a few seconds to mix. open the lid slowly to let the carbonation out.
